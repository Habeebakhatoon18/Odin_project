Tea/Chai

Description

Tea/Chai is a milky Indian tea drink made with black tea, mill and optional sugar and spices. Chai is not just a drink, it is an emotion. Chai time or tea time is extremely sacred for most Indian families.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups Milk
  • 1 cup water
  • 6 teaspoon sugar
  • 3 teaspoon tea mixture powder
  • ginger(optional)

Steps

  1. Take a small vessel add 2 cup milk and 1 cup water and boil for 1-2 minutes
  2. Once milk boiled .Add sugar and boil for 30 sec again
  3. now add tea powder (ginger optional)and boil till you see a dark colour
  4. Now the strain tea in cups and enjoy!
